How U'ocr Works

Powered by Advanced AI Technology

U'ocr uses artificial intelligence to "learn" how to read any handwriting. For this, the software first needed to be trained with a number of pages that have been manually transcribed. This "Ground Truth" enables U'ocr to understand and read any form of handwriting.

The model "Universal Giant" was trained with more than 15 million words from many different handwritings, allowing it to recognize diverse handwriting styles with remarkable accuracy.

About Handwriting

Preserving Historical Heritage

Handwriting has been a primary form of communication across cultures for centuries. Millions of documents, letters, and personal notes exist, penned in various handwriting styles. With U'ocr, these documents can be digitized and made accessible for public or private use.

Whether you're a historian working with ancient manuscripts, a genealogist deciphering family records, or simply someone with a collection of handwritten notes, U'ocr provides the tools to convert these valuable documents into searchable, editable text.

Our technology supports multiple languages and various handwriting styles, from beautiful calligraphy to hastily scribbled notes, making it versatile for all your handwriting recognition needs.
